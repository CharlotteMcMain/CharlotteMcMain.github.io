A block of wood is floating in calm water.
The density of the wood is $700\ \text{kg m}^{-3}$. The density of water is $1000\  \text{kg m}^{−3}$
What percentage of the volume of the block is **above** the waterline?

Options:
a - 30
b - 50
c - 70
d - 89

## Explanation
##### Identify
- density of wood, $\rho_\text{wood}=700 \text{kg m}^{-3}$
- density of water, $\rho_\text{water}=1000 \text{kg m}^{-3}$
- *"...block of wood is **floating** in calm water"* - this tells us by **Archimede's Principle**: An object in a fluid experiences an upthrust (buoyant force) equal to the weight of the fluid it displaces. When it is floating, this displaced volume, is therefore the submerged volume
- **Target Variable:** percentage of block above waterline

##### Set-up
- **Upthrust:**
$$F=\rho g V$$
where:
- $V$ = volume (i.e. liquid) displaced

- **Weight:**
$$W=mg$$

##### Execute
Since it is floating, the object is in equilibrium (net force is zero):
i.e., the upward force form the water (upthrust) equals the downward weight of the block
$$F_\text{upthrust} = m_\text{block}g=\rho_\text{block}V_\text{block}g$$
Archimedes' principle tells us that:
$$F_\text{upthrust}=W_\text{displaced water}=m_\text{water}g=\rho_\text{water}  V_\text{submerged}g$$
So:
$$\rho_\text{water} V_\text{submerged}g = \rho_\text{block}V_\text{block}g$$$$\rho_\text{water} V_\text{submerged} = \rho_\text{block}V_\text{block}$$$$\frac{V_\text{submerged}}{V_\text{block}}=\frac{\rho_\text{block}}{\rho_\text{water}}=\frac{700}{1000}=0.7$$
This fraction asks: "What fraction of the block's total volume is submerged?". Therefore, this tells us that $70\%$ is submerged, and therefore, $(1-0.7=)\ 30\%$ is above the waterline.
## Mark scheme
A
**Examiner's Comments:**
For the block to float, its weight (700 x V x g) must equal the weight of water displaced (1000 x k x V x g) where k is the fraction of the block under water. Equating these two expressions gives k as 0.7. The question, however, asks for the percentage of the block above the waterline, which is clearly 0.3, making A the correct answer.
