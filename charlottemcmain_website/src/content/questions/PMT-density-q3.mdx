### Question

A spring has a force constant of $4900\ \text{N m}^{-1}$. 

A force is applied to the spring, causing it to compress by $0.50\ \text{m}$.

What is the change in the elastic potential energy stored in the spring?

### Options

- (A) decreases by $610$ J
- (B) decreases by $1200$ J
- (C) increases by $610$ J
- (D) increases by $1200$ J

### Answer

C

### Explanation

##### Identify
- By compressing the spring, we are doing work on the spring (+ve W)

##### Set-up
$$ E=\frac{1}{2}kx^2$$

##### Execute
$$E=\frac{1}{2}\times4900\times0.5^2=610\ \text{J}$$

### Mark scheme

**Answer: C**
