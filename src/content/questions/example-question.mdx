### Question

A particle accelerates uniformly. Which equation is correct?

### Options
- (A) $v = u - at$
- (B) $v = u + at$
- (C) $s = ut - \tfrac12 at^2$
- (D) $a = \dfrac{u}{t}$

### Answer
B

### Explanation

Uniform acceleration gives:

$$
v = u + at
$$
