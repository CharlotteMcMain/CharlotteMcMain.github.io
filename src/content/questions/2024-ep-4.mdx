  <b>Rules for Capacitors:</b>
  - **Parallel:** $C_\text{total} = C_1+C_2+...$
  - **Series:** $\frac{1}{C_\text{total}} = \frac{1}{C_1}+\frac{1}{C_2}+...$

  Therefore:
  - **In the top branch:**
  $$\frac{1}{C_\text{series}}=\frac{1}{0.33}+\frac{1}{0.22}=7.58$$
  
  Therefore: $C_\text{series}=\frac{1}{7.58}=0.132\ \mu\text{F}$

  $$ C_\text{total}=0.132+0.47=0.602\ \mu\text{F}$$
