Kilowatt-hour ($\text{kWh}$) means: $1000 \text{W} \times 1 \text{hour}$
Watts are joules per second: $1 \text{ W} = 1 \dfrac{\text{J}}{\text{s}}$
A joule in SI units is: $1 \text{ J} = 1 \text{ kg }\text{m}^{2}\text{ s}^{-2}$
Converting 1 hour into seconds: $1 \text{ hour} = 3600 \text{ s}$
Therefore $1\text{ kWh}$ becomes: $\text{kg m}^2 \text{s}^{-2}$
